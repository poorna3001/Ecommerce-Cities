How to Register as a Seller

Sign Up & Create Your Store

  • Fill out a simple registration form with your business details.
  • Business Name & Contact Information
  • Store Address & Business Type
  • GSTIN (if applicable)
  • No GST? No problem! We support small businesses & individual sellers too!

List Your Products in Minutes

  • Upload products with images, descriptions, and pricing.
  • Add product variations (size, color, weight, etc.)
  • Set stock levels and availability
  • Enable special discounts & offers

Start Selling & Receive Orders

  • Once approved, your store goes live!
  • Customers can browse and buy your products instantly
  • Manage orders easily via the seller dashboard
  • Get real-time notifications for new orders

Deliver with Our Logistics Support

  • Choose from trusted local delivery partners or manage your own shipping
  • On-demand pickup for hassle-free logistics
  • Real-time tracking for sellers & buyers
  • Cash-on-Delivery (COD) option to boost sales

Get Paid Securely & Grow Your Business

  • Receive direct bank transfers from online payments
  • Weekly payouts for a smooth cash flow
  • Secure payment gateway for safe transactions
  • Seller analytics to track sales & earnings
